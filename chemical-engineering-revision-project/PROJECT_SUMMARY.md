# Chemical Engineering Revision Project - Summary

## Project Overview
This comprehensive 7-day revision project has been created to prepare chemical engineering fundamentals for IIM interviews. The project connects technical knowledge to business applications and provides structured daily plans for effective preparation.

## Files Created

1. **README.md** - Project overview and objectives
2. **SCHEDULE_OVERVIEW.md** - Detailed timeline and daily structure
3. **day1_process_engineering.md** - Process engineering fundamentals
4. **day2_thermodynamics_heat_transfer.md** - Thermodynamics and heat transfer
5. **day3_mass_transfer_operations.md** - Mass transfer and separations
6. **day4_reaction_engineering_catalysis.md** - Reaction engineering and catalysis
7. **day5_process_control_optimization.md** - Process control and optimization
8. **day6_safety_environment_economics.md** - Safety, environment, and economics
9. **day7_integration_mock_interview.md** - Integration and mock interview prep
10. **quick_reference_guide.md** - Last-minute review guide

## Key Features

### Comprehensive Coverage
- All major chemical engineering domains covered
- Business connections emphasized throughout
- Interview-focused examples and questions provided

### Structured Approach
- 7-day plan with specific daily objectives
- Morning/afternoon/evening activities scheduled
- Success metrics defined for each day

### Practical Application
- Real-world examples integrated throughout
- Sample interview questions and responses
- Industry trends and key phrases included

## How to Use This Project

1. Follow the 7-day schedule sequentially
2. Spend approximately 6-8 hours per day on the material
3. Practice explaining concepts aloud
4. Connect technical information to business value
5. Prepare specific examples from your experience
6. Use the quick reference guide for final review

## Success Metrics
Upon completing this project, you should be able to:
- Confidently discuss technical concepts with non-technical interviewers
- Articulate the business value of engineering decisions
- Provide specific examples of technical leadership
- Demonstrate understanding of industry challenges
- Connect your technical background to management aspirations

## Best of Luck!
This project provides a comprehensive foundation for your IIM interviews. Remember to practice explaining concepts simply, focus on business impact, and connect your technical expertise to your management goals.
