# Chemical Engineering Revision Project for IIM Interviews

## Overview
This 7-day intensive revision project is designed to prepare chemical engineering fundamentals for IIM interviews. The focus is on key concepts that are commonly discussed in management interviews, connecting technical knowledge to business applications.

## Objectives
- Review core chemical engineering principles
- Understand how chemical engineering connects to business and management
- Prepare technical examples for case studies and behavioral questions
- Develop ability to explain complex concepts simply

## Daily Schedule Overview
- **Day 1**: Process Engineering Fundamentals
- **Day 2**: Thermodynamics & Heat Transfer
- **Day 3**: Mass Transfer Operations
- **Day 4**: Reaction Engineering & Catalysis
- **Day 5**: Process Control & Optimization
- **Day 6**: Safety, Environment & Economics
- **Day 7**: Integration & Mock Interview Prep
