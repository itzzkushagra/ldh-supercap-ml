# Day 2: Thermodynamics & Heat Transfer

## Learning Objectives
- Review fundamental thermodynamic laws and properties
- Understand phase equilibrium and property estimation
- Master heat transfer mechanisms and equipment
- Connect energy efficiency to business value

## Key Topics to Review

### Thermodynamics Principles
- First and second laws of thermodynamics
- Property relationships (enthalpy, entropy, Gibbs free energy)
- Phase equilibria and equations of state
- Thermodynamic cycles (Rankine, refrigeration, etc.)

### Heat Transfer
- Conduction, convection, and radiation
- Heat exchanger types and design
- Overall heat transfer coefficients
- Fouling and maintenance considerations

### Business Connection Points
- Energy efficiency improvements and cost savings
- Utility systems optimization
- Waste heat recovery opportunities
- Environmental impact of energy consumption

## Daily Tasks
1. Practice solving thermodynamic property calculations
2. Review heat exchanger sizing procedures
3. Identify energy saving opportunities in common processes
4. Prepare examples of energy efficiency projects

## Interview Preparation Questions
- How would you improve energy efficiency in a chemical plant?
- Explain the concept of entropy in simple terms
- What are the economic implications of thermodynamic efficiency?
- Describe a situation where you optimized energy usage

## Resources for Review
- Thermodynamics chapters in standard textbooks
- Heat Transfer by Incropera & DeWitt
- Energy audit case studies
