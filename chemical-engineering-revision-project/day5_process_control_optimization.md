# Day 5: Process Control & Optimization

## Learning Objectives
- Understand process control fundamentals and strategies
- Learn about process optimization techniques
- Connect automation to operational excellence
- Prepare examples of process improvement initiatives

## Key Topics to Review

### Process Control Systems
- Feedback and feedforward control
- PID controllers and tuning methods
- Control valve selection and sizing
- Distributed control systems (DCS)

### Advanced Control
- Cascade and ratio control
- Model predictive control (MPC)
- Statistical process control
- Safety instrumented systems (SIS)

### Process Optimization
- Linear and nonlinear programming
- Economic optimization targets
- Constraint handling
- Real-time optimization (RTO)

### Business Connection Points
- Impact of control on product quality and consistency
- Cost benefits of advanced control systems
- Reliability and availability improvements
- Operator efficiency and training

## Daily Tasks
1. Review control system design principles
2. Study common control strategies for unit operations
3. Research case studies of process optimization projects
4. Prepare examples of automation benefits in industry

## Interview Preparation Questions
- How does good process control impact business metrics?
- What's the difference between regulatory and advanced control?
- How would you approach optimizing an existing process?
- Describe the relationship between control and safety

## Resources for Review
- Process Dynamics and Control by Seborg, Edgar & Mellichamp
- Chemical Process Control by Stephanopoulos
- APC/Advanced Process Control case studies
