# 7-Day Chemical Engineering Revision Schedule for IIM Interviews

## Complete Timeline

### Day 1: Process Engineering Fundamentals
**Focus**: Unit operations, process design, material balances
- Morning: Review unit operations (fluid flow, heat exchangers, separation)
- Afternoon: Practice PFDs and equipment sizing
- Evening: Prepare examples of process optimization

### Day 2: Thermodynamics & Heat Transfer
**Focus**: Energy balances, property relationships, efficiency
- Morning: Review thermodynamic laws and cycles
- Afternoon: Heat transfer mechanisms and exchanger design
- Evening: Prepare energy efficiency examples

### Day 3: Mass Transfer Operations
**Focus**: Separation processes, mass transfer principles
- Morning: Distillation theory and practice
- Afternoon: Other separation methods (absorption, extraction, etc.)
- Evening: Prepare separation optimization examples

### Day 4: Reaction Engineering & Catalysis
**Focus**: Reactor design, kinetics, catalysts
- Morning: Reaction kinetics and reactor types
- Afternoon: Catalysis principles and applications
- Evening: Prepare reactor innovation examples

### Day 5: Process Control & Optimization
**Focus**: Automation, control systems, optimization
- Morning: Basic control theory and PID controllers
- Afternoon: Advanced control and optimization techniques
- Evening: Prepare automation benefit examples

### Day 6: Safety, Environment & Economics
**Focus**: Risk assessment, sustainability, financial analysis
- Morning: Process safety and risk management
- Afternoon: Environmental regulations and economics
- Evening: Prepare safety/sustainability examples

### Day 7: Integration & Mock Interview Prep
**Focus**: Synthesis, practice, final preparation
- Morning: Knowledge integration and mind mapping
- Afternoon: Mock interviews and feedback
- Evening: Final review and confidence building

## Daily Structure Template
- **9:00-10:30 AM**: Core concept review
- **10:30-10:45 AM**: Break
- **10:45-12:15 PM**: Applications and examples
- **12:15-1:15 PM**: Lunch break
- **1:15-2:45 PM**: Business connection and case studies
- **2:45-3:00 PM**: Break
- **3:00-4:30 PM**: Interview preparation and practice
- **4:30-5:00 PM**: Summary and tomorrow prep

## Success Metrics for Each Day
After completing each day, ensure you can:
1. Explain the key concepts simply to a non-technical person
2. Connect the topic to business value/impact
3. Provide at least one concrete example from industry or your experience
4. Answer basic technical questions confidently
5. Relate the concepts to current industry trends

## Tips for Maximum Effectiveness
- Focus on understanding over memorization
- Practice explaining concepts aloud
- Connect each topic to business outcomes
- Prepare specific examples from your experience
- Stay updated on industry trends and challenges
- Maintain a positive attitude and confidence in your expertise
